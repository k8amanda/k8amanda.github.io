Biography

Hello, my name is Kate Hanson. I was born and raised in Long Beach, New York. Currently I am a rising sophomore at Tufts University in Medford, MA. My major is Computer Science within the School of Engineering and my minor is Engineering Management. I am also the Social Chair for our school's Women in Computer Science (WiCS) organization.

I graduated from Long Beach High School in 2017 with an Internation Baccalaureate degree. I was the president of our Literary Club and would lead the members in in-depth discussions about what we were reading as well as organizing book drives. I was the editor of our yearbook and took the majority of the pictures that were used. In addition, I introduced an annual student-faculty game titled "Basketball for EB" where we donated all proceeds to EB Research Partnership. This is a non-profit organization I have volunteered for since I was twelve.

I enjoy going to the beach with my family and taking road trips with my friends. A few of my favorites include sitcoms, anything chocolate, giraffes, and the color blue. I love doing anything artistic including dancing, writing, drawing, and playing the flute. Someday I hope to follow a creative path within the field of Computer Science.
